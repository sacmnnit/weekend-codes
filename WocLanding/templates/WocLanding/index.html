Get fresh insights into the world of Programming and Development

WEEKEND OF CODE is a joint initiative of B.Tech final and pre-final year students to provide the freshers an exposure to the world of competitive programming alongwith a glimpse of software development. The Weekend Of Code basically consists of two events:

  • CodeStart: CodeStart is a 3 hours ACM-ICPC styled onsite coding contest. The event aims at providing freshers an experience equivalent to participating in international coding event.
  •       DevJam: DevJam is 24 hour onsite development event which aims at familiarising the freshers with the latest frameworks and technologies in IT industry. This event will allow the freshers to learn new technologies and will groom them for participating in various national and international events and thus, take the name of college to new heights.

Why WOC

Near 20 teams applied for Hack36 but only 2 of them got the
chance to participate in the main event due the wide participation of teams from
other colleges. This wide participation of the first year students depicts their enthusiasm
and inclination towards development activities.
After not getting a chance in Hack36 they felt a little discouraged. This event is an attempt
to motivate them to continue their zest to learn and develop more.

Google Summer Of Code

The best time to make the students aware about the procedure of preparation and selection for such kind of programmes is the first year. GSoC is a great opportunity for students to contribute to open source .

Practice For ACM ICPC

The ACM-ICPC is considered as the "Olympics of Programming Competitions". It is quite simply, the oldest, largest, and most prestigious international level programming contest. MNNIT is good , we need to make it best.

Betterment of Placement Statistics

CodeStart and DevJam will help them improve their algorithmic skills and expanding their development skill for for companies. This will reflect in the placement statistics of the college.
